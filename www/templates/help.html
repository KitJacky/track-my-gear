What can I do with this App?

You can add your iBeacons and get a notification when the beacon goes out of range and see its last known location. Or get a notification when you enter the region of a beacon.

With Nearby Eddystones you can view all Eddystone beacons within range, sorted by distance.

What is the distance of a Beacon?

The range of a beacon depends on the beacon itself. Some beacons have a 100 meter range and some a 30 meter.

Use Cases

Add a beacon to your keys so you don't forget them.
Add a beacon in your wallet so you don't forget it.
Give a beacon to your kids so they stay in range.
Get notified when your pet goes out of range.
Add a beacon in your car so you get a notification when it's in range or see where you parked it.
Give beacons away as a promotional gift.

Troubleshooting

Make sure Bluetooth is turned on.
Make sure this app has permissions to send notifications.
When a beacon is out of range, it can take 10 seconds before you receive a notification.
